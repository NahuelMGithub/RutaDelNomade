What is the Nomad's Route?

Nomad's Route is the ideal platform for digital nomads. We offer a personalized journey with several stops in different cities of the country, where you can stay as long as you want. Each city on our route is equipped with all the essential services you need to work remotely: coworking, accommodation, gastronomy, and recreational activities. Additionally, we facilitate payments with cryptocurrencies and have a local team that will assist you at all times, connecting you with tourist options and outdoor activities to enjoy while working. At the end of your stay in one city, simply continue your journey to the next stop on your route, worry-free. Discover a new lifestyle, work from the best places, and enjoy a unique experience while exploring the best of Argentina.

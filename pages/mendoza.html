Mendoza Capital

Experience in Mendoza Capital

In Mendoza Capital, you will enjoy a unique experience that combines nature, adventure, and culture. Here are some activities you can't miss:

  • General San Mart칤n Park: A monumental green space ideal for outdoor walks, picnics, and exploring the artificial lake and Cerro de la Gloria.
  • Rafting in the Mendoza River: Feel the thrill of rafting in the waters of the Mendoza River, a perfect destination for adventure sports enthusiasts.
  • Wine Route by Bicycle: Cycle through Mendoza's vineyards, enjoy guided tours of wineries, and finish with a tasting of world-class wines.
  • Catena Zapata Winery Tour: Visit one of the most famous wineries in the region and participate in a tour where you'll learn about winemaking and enjoy an exclusive tasting.
  • Hiking at Cerro Arco: If you love hiking, Cerro Arco is one of the best spots to enjoy a panoramic view of Mendoza and the Andes Mountains.
  • Visit the Historic Center: Take a tour through the city's streets, visiting places like Independence Plaza, Defense Plaza, and Mendoza Cathedral.
  • Excursion to Potrerillos: Just a few kilometers from Mendoza Capital, Potrerillos offers activities like boating, fishing, and hiking amidst breathtaking scenery.

After a day of activities, relax in the best hotels and coworks in the city, such as the Diplomatic Hotel, with views of the Andes, or enjoy the modern space of Coffice Mendoza, ideal for working while enjoying the city.
